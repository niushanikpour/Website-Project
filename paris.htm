Paris


Shabestan

Shabestan is on Champs-Elysee in Paris. the location is great and super central to the busiest parts of Paris. The interior is pretty modern but in a European way since it still has that historic feel. The bar is the most interesting part. I put a photo of it below; it looks like a gold bird is at the end of it. The have all the basics here as well, and the portion size is average but shareable. I would not say that there is a wide range of options, but they do have the basics. They also have the Persian ice cream that I like, so that is a plus. The third photo in the slideshow below (after the image of the kabob dish) is what a Shabestan means in English.
