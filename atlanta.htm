Divan

Divan is one of the largest restaurants on this list in terms of size. It has four floors, but each floor offers something different (hookah lounge, bar, dining areas). I would say that Divan has good food but is not the most authentic as they have many dishes that may have some Persian aspects but are not really Persian at all. The food is unique and creative, and there is something there for everyone. I have not been in over five years so they have changed since I have been. They have shifted to a more Mediterranean-inspired menu since then. The mezze plates are particularly delicious, like the hummus and baba ganoush. If you like seafood, the octopus is also really good. Their lamb shank with saffron is good and one of the more traditional dishes they have; another one I like is their salmon dish (which is more Mediterranean than anything else). This place is great to get a few different plates and share or order your own main dish. They can get pretty busy so if you are going with a large group I would suggest making a reservation.
