Iran


Nayeb Saei Restaurant

Nayeb Saei is a well-known restaurant in Tehran, I have never been there but my parents have been many times. It has traditional Persian food in a more luxurious atmosphere. The outside and inside are really beautiful too. It has a wide range of dishes on their menu so you can try all the classics. They are known to be consistent; they have been open for around two decades and still have the same quality. The portions are big which is why they are perfect to share with a group. If you are looking for a higher-end feeling restaurant without super expensive prices, thisplace is perfect. I am not too sure on price because it is in a different currency but it is mid-level priced.

  1. Atmosphere
    • Location: 5/5
    • Look/Interior: 4.5/5
    • Service: 4.5/5
  2. Price: 4/5
  3. Food
    • Variety: 3.8/5
    • Quality: 4/5
    • Overall: 4.3/5

Alborz Restaurant

Alborz is a huge restaurant in Iran, both my parents are super familiar with this place. My dad goes at least twice everytime he goes to Iran. The restaurant is named after the Alborz mountain range, which is located to the north of Tehran. Since it’s in Iran it is most definitely traditional. The menu has every Iranian dish you could ask for. The portions are big and the energy there is to come in with a group, enjoy your company and share food together. The quality of the food is amazing and the interior is really nice as well. The service is great and they will help you find a dish that best fits what you are looking for. I am not too sure on the price because it is in a different currency as well.

  1. Atmosphere
    • Location: 5/5
    • Look/Interior: 4/5
    • Service: 5/5
  2. Price: 4/5
  3. Food
    • Variety: 3/5
    • Quality: 4/5
    • Overall: 4.2/5

Shandiz

Shandiz has multiple locations in Iran, there is at least one in Tehran and Mashhad. The main thing to get here is meat and kabobs. The inside is really pretty and decorated. It almost looks like a palace inside. It definitely adds to the experience of eating there. The meat is really good quality and cooked/seasoned well. Like the other restaurants in Iran, it is definitely as authentic as you can get. It’s great to finish your meal here with dessert and Persian tea. I am not too sure on the price because it is in a different currency as well, but I know it is more mid-priced, which is a great deal for how nice the place is.
