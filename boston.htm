Boston (Watertown)


Roksana's

This place is only really good for take-out, especially for families and bigger groups in general but they also have single orders. They offer family style meals, so you can get a bunch of dishes in really large quantities. You can buy any dips or sides you want, and can even choose the amount because you pay by the pound. Their rice, salads, dips, and meats are good and pretty traditional. It is mainly run by a Persian family and they are super sweet and willing to help if you do not know what to get or how much to get. They have a little market inside as well so you can buy other Persian snacks, foods, drinks, honestly everything. They also have a deli counter if you want a specific amount of dips or salads. This restaurant is on a street that has a lot of other Persian things; there is a big Persian market nearby and next door is an ice cream shop that sells Persian inspired flavors.
