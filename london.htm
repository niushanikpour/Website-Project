London


Iran Restaurant

This is one of the most popular Iranian restaurants in London. They have a large variety of salads, meats, appetizers, desserts, etc. The portions are pretty big but I would say it is not meant for family style eating, you could share mains with one other person. Everything here is good and worth a try. They have a good amount of vegetarian options as well. Most main courses come with rice and salad too. There is a lot of seating so you could come with large groups, maybe just call ahead. They also give each person tea at the end of their meal. The cost is around £35-45 a person.

Tajrish Restaurant

Tajrish is a family-owned and runned restaurant. The inside is small but they also offer take-out as well. The prices here are really great, especially because the portions are so big. The menu is not big but they have all the typical and traditional dishes. The teapots and teacups they use are cute and the tea inside is delicious as well. The best thing about this restaurant definitely has to be the value of what you are getting; it is not expensive and the portions are huge. The dip sampler is a must. The cost is about £20 a person.

Diba

The inside of this restaurant is beautiful, they did a great job of having it be inspired by Middle-Eastern decor but really modern and just a nice place to be in. It has vines hanging from the ceiling, brick walls, and really pretty lights. They have good deals for groups, so you can order something for four people and it is cheaper.They have a lot of traditional dishes but also have a spin on them which is a unique difference. The cost is about £25-30 a person. But for this price, the atmosphere of the restaurant is amazing.
